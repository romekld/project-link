A. Monthly Program and Mortality FOURmula One (F1) Plus Indicators 
Report (M2 Sections B and C) 
 
The Monthly Program and Mortality F1 Plus Indicators Report (M2 Sections B and C)  
is prepared by the midwife at the barangay level. The midwife fills up the form to 
report the coverage of F1 Plus indicators from the first day to the last day of the 
month. This is then submitted to the RHU /MHC. At the RHU/MHC, the PHN 
consolidates all the submitted M2s and generates the RHU/MHC Report using the M2 
for the RHU/MHC. This is submitted to the PHO/CHO. The M2 forms are also used by 
the PHO/CHOs to submit reports to DOH-CHDs then the DOH-CHDs use the same 
forms to submit to the DOH-CO. 
 
How to Accomplish the Monthly Report on FOURmula One (F1) Plus Indicators 
 
1. The M2 Report for F1 Plus Indicators is divided into 2 sections with corresponding 
indicators to be reported: 
 
Section B. Program F1 Plus Indicators 
Section C. Mortality F1 Plus Indicators 
2. Column 1 lists the indicators to be reported. 
 
3. Disaggregate the coverage into male and female under Columns 2 and 3 and write 
the total under Column 4.  
 
  
  
This Monthly Program and Mortality FOURmula One (F1) Plus 
Indicators Report shall be incorporated into the overall M2 Report 
to be submitted by the health facility to the next administrative 
level together with the Monthly Morbidity Report (M2)

B. Recording and Reporting Selected Child Care and Infectious Disease 
Indicators Using the Annual Report Form (A1 Sections A and B) 
 
There are selected Child Care and Infectious Disease indicators which will be  
recorded and reported through the Annual Report Form (A1 Sections A and B). These 
data are submitted once year. 
 
The Annual Report on Selected Child Care and Infectious Disease Indicators  is the 
official health report of the barangay/municipality/city for the year. The midwife fills up 
the form to report the data of the year. This is submitted to the nurse at the RHU/MHC 
for consolidation. The PHN forwards this report to the Provincial FHSIS Coordinator at 
the PHO for provincial consolidation. The municipality/city prepares only one annual 
report. In case there is more than one RHU/MHC in the municipality/city, the 
MHO/CHO shall be responsible for directing the consolidation of all the annu al data 
from different RHUs/MHCs and the preparation of one Annual Report for the 
municipality/city.  
 
How to Accomplish the Annual Report on Demographic Data 
1. Column 1 lists the indicators to be reported 
 
2. Disaggregate the data to male or female. 
 
3. Write the total or sum of the counts for each indicator under the Total column. 
 
4. Compute for the rate or proportion  of each indicator  based on the formula per 
indicator in the metadata and write the result on the Rate column. 
 
5. Analyze the data and write the interpretation and Recommendations/Actions to 
be Taken under the appropriate column. 
This Annual Report on Selected Child Care and Infectious Disease 
Indicators shall be incorporated into the overall Annual Report (A1) to be 
submitted by the health facility to the higher administration together with 
the Demographic Data.
