A. Scope of Infectious Diseases Prevention and Control Services 
 
The FHSIS records and reports services provided for the prevention and control of 
infectious diseases. These Infectious Disease Prevention and Control Programs are 
grouped into two: Group A are those that depend largely on the FHSIS in tracking 
their program accomplishments while Group B includes those with their own -
established information system.  These two groups are shown below with the specific 
services to be tracked and reported.

provider shall:

6. Record the management and treatment administered.

E. Reporting on a Monthly and Quarterly Basis 
 
E.1 Monthly Program Service Coverage Report (M1) 
 
The Monthly Program Service Coverage Report is prepared by the midwife at the 
barangay level. The midwife fills up the M1 Form to report the program service 
coverage from the first day to the last day of the month. She submits this to the nurse 
at the RHU/MHC for consolidation. Spaces are left blank  for those indicators the 
municipality/city needs to generate at their level. 
 
(1) Based on the accomplished Summary Table  on the different p rograms, 
transfer the data to the M onthly Report, segregating the data by male and 
female. 
 
E.2 Quarterly Program Service Coverage Report (Q1) 
The Quarterly Program Service Coverage Report is the official health report of the 
municipality/city for the quarter. It contains the consolidated three month reports from 
all the BHSs and RHU/MHC during the quarter. The PHN forwards this report to the 
Provincial FHSIS Coordinator at the PHO every third week of the first month of the 
succeeding quarter  for provincial consolidation. The municipality/city prepares only 
one quarterly report.  In case there is more than one RHU/MHC in the 
municipality/city, the MHO/CHO shall be responsible for directing the  consolidation of 
all the quarterly data from different RHUs/MHCs and the preparation of one Quarterly 
Report for the municipality/city.  
 
(1) For the Q1 Report on the Infectious Disease Prevention and Control, 
consolidate the Monthly Accomplishment Report to obtain the quarter totals.  
 
(2) Refer at the denominators column  of each set of indicators in computing the 
rates/percentages.  
 
(3) Based on the generated reports on ITIS, PhilMIS, ILIS and NaRIS, transfer the 
data for each listed indicator in Q1 for the TB, Mal aria, Leprosy and Rabies 
Programs. Otherwise, use the MCTs if the above -mentioned information 
systems are non-functional in the barangay/municipality. 
 
(4) Analyze the results of the Quarterly Program Accomplishment and write these 
under Column 6 while providing recommendations under Column 7.  
 
  
Note that M1 and Q1 Reports on the accomplishment of the Infectious 
Disease Prevention and Control Program shall form part of the Overall 
M1 and Q1 Reports to be submitted by the Health Facility FHSIS Staff 
to the next level of administration together with the other M1 and Q1 
Reports of the Other Public Health Programs,
